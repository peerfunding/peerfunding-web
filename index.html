Discover the Power of PeerFunding

PeerFunding operates on a trusted rotational contribution system where an organization facilitates the pooling of funds among a group of participants. Each cycle continues until every participant has received their fair share of the pooled funds. The system ensures fairness, accountability, and financial stability among its members.

To safeguard the integrity of active contribution cycles, PeerFunding includes an insurance fee.
The insurance fee is refundable after a successful completion of the contribution cycle.

The participants give their consent to contribute an agreed-upon amount regularly and receive a lump sum at their designated turn, allowing them to plan and manage their financial obligations efficiently. This system ensures that members can handle major expenses without stress, fostering a disciplined and collective approach to saving.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our PeerFunding.

How can I sign up for an account?

To sign up, click on the 'Download' button on the homepage, after installing PeerFunding, follow the instructions to create your account.

What types of subscription plans do you offer?

We offer Free, Pay-Per-Use, Standard, and Premium plans for both organizations and participants, each with varying features and benefits. You can choose a plan that best suits your needs.

How can I set up my contribution account?

To set up you contribution account, you will provide your BVN during registration for PeerFunding to create an active bank account for you. You can use the Pay-In or Pay-Out buttons on the account to transfer funds into and out of your account.

How can I browse contributions from other users?

You can explore contributions from other users by browsing through the platform's content sections or using search filters to find specific topics or projects.

How do I track my contributions?

You can track your contributions through the contribution page, which will show your activity and progress of the active contribution

Can the contribution be suspended?

Yes, If PeerFunding is unable to debit the account of any participant, the contribution will be suspended for the organizer to resolve the issue and request for reactivation and the participant is informed for the violation of PeerFunding Contribution Policy.

Can I organize a contribution?

Yes, once you set up an organization and subscribe to the organization subscription plan, you can manage contributions for a group of participants while ensuring accountability and timely..

What happens if an issue arises in the contribution?

PeerFunding has includes an Insurance Protection Mechanism. If a contribution completes successfully, the insurance fee is refunded. However, if there is an unresolved issue within 72 hours, the fee is non-refundable.

Is my money safe on PeerFunding?

Yes, PeerFunding maintains an active bank account with a partner bank for each participant funds. Again, PeerFunding also encourages funding your account a day or 2 before the contribution payment due date.

How can I pay for my active contribution on PeerFunding?

To pay for active contribution, just fund your account. PeerFunding will deducted the contribution amount from your account on the contribution due day (payment date).

Can I change my subscription plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time based on your needs and level of participation.

When do I need to fund my account?

Ensure you account is funded on or before the contribution payment due date across all your active contributions.
